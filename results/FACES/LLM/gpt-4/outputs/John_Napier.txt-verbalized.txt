< pad > John Napier was born on February 1, 1550 in Edinburgh and lived in Merchiston Castle. He was a Math profesional and attended the University of St Andrews. He was married to Elizabeth Stirling and had a notable work at the Mirifici Logarithmorum Canonis Descriptio. Napier was also an academician and was awarded the Gouden Ganzenveer award. < / s >
