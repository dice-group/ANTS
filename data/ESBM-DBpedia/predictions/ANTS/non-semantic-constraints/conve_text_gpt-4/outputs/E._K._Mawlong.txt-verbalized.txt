< pad > E. K. Mawlong was born on Aug 20, 1951 in Umpling, and died in Shillong on Sept 17, 2008. He was a member of the U.S. Democratic Party. His profession was politician, and his employer was the Speaker of the Meghalaya Legislative Assembly. His daughter was named Kitboklang Kharshiing. K. Mawlong was awarded the Padma Shri, and his successor was Flinder Anderson Khonglam. < / s >
